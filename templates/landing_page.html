Is SignLingo free to use?

Yes! The core learning curriculum is completely free and accessible to everyone. We believe in removing financial barriers to education. For users who want an ad-free experience and access to advanced features, we offer a premium subscription at IDR 49,900/month.

How does the AI feedback system work?

Our proprietary AI uses your device's camera to analyze your signing in real-time. As you practice, our hybrid CNN-LSTM model (with 95.30% accuracy) compares your hand movements to the correct sign and provides immediate feedback on your accuracy, helping you improve with every practice session.

Do I need any prior experience to start learning?

Not at all! SignLingo is designed for learners of all levels. Our structured curriculum starts with the basics and gradually progresses to advanced signs. Whether you're a complete beginner or looking to improve your existing skills, our adaptive learning system adjusts to your pace.

How is SignLingo different from watching video tutorials?

Unlike passive video tutorials, SignLingo provides active, interactive learning. You don't just watch—you practice with your camera and receive real-time AI feedback on your technique. This interactive feedback loop helps you truly master signs, not just memorize them.
